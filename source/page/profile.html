ATELIERS CHEN ZEQIANG is a creative collective that performs design direction and design. Together with clients and talented collaborators, we are exploring contemporary language that explains the way the world is currently expressing itself, by using technology and design. Our aims are to investigate societal trends in design, similar to how think tanks do research, and to make a difference in daily life through achievements in design.
